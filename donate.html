Donation levels

Make the impact concrete.

Choose a general gift to Vatandosh, or designate your gift for Terra Vita by writing "Terra Vita" in the PayPal note.

$25

Supports basic community needs, including education materials, family support, or animal care supplies.

$50

Helps fund practical needs such as veterinary support for Terra Vita, student resources, or local training materials.

$100

Contributes to humanitarian and social protection projects supporting vulnerable families, local partners, and community service providers.

$250+

Supports larger project needs, including shelter support, research, training, convenings, and multi-year partnerships.

For Terra Vita animal welfare donations, use the PayPal button and write "Terra Vita" in the note. For monthly gifts, write "Terra Vita monthly" or contact us for help setting it up.
