Privacy Policy

Vatandosh Connect respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This website may collect limited information such as name, email address, and contact details when users submit forms, subscribe to updates, volunteer, donate, or contact us.

Information collected through this website is used solely for communication, program updates, donor and partner follow-up, and improving our services. We do not sell personal data or share it with third parties except when required by law or necessary to process secure donations.

Donations made through third-party platforms such as PayPal are processed according to those platforms' privacy and security policies. Substack subscriptions are handled by Substack according to its platform policies.

To ask questions about this policy or request changes to contact information we hold, email info@vatandosh.org.
