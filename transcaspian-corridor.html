Strategic initiative

TransCaspian Corridor

A new Vatandosh platform for research, convening, and public education on the Middle Corridor connecting Central Asia, the Caspian region, the South Caucasus, Turkey, and Europe.

Discuss Partnership Read Analysis
Why it matters

Uzbekistan's global future depends on trusted connections.

The TransCaspian Corridor is more than a trade route. It is a strategic development question touching infrastructure, education, regional cooperation, supply chains, public policy, and the role of diaspora expertise.

This project can position Vatandosh Connect as a bridge between policy thinkers, logistics experts, universities, civic leaders, and entrepreneurs interested in Uzbekistan's place in Eurasian connectivity.

Program design

Three practical workstreams

This gives the project a professional frame even before all partners and deliverables are finalized.

01

Research & Briefings

Publish accessible explainers, policy notes, and expert interviews on the corridor's implications for Uzbekistan and Central Asia.

02

Convenings

Host roundtables with diaspora professionals, universities, logistics leaders, policymakers, and regional experts.

03

Talent Pipeline

Connect students and professionals with learning opportunities in trade, infrastructure, public policy, and regional studies.
