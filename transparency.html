Programs are organized around education, social protection, and institutional development.

Vatandosh Connect supports initiatives that improve opportunity, wellbeing, and resilience for Uzbek communities through international partnerships and local implementation.

  • Education and workforce development, including engineering education and modern skills pathways.
  • Social protection work including poverty reduction, domestic violence prevention, children's rights, and autism support.
  • Public policy, fiscal transparency, cultural exchange, and research collaboration.
  • Community engagement projects, including animal protection and humane-treatment awareness.
