Grant-ready posture

Designed for trust, eligibility, and clear public benefit.

For Google for Nonprofits and Google Ad Grants, the site needs a clear mission, transparent nonprofit status, useful public content, working navigation, and no placeholder language. The site is structured around those requirements.

  • Visible 501(c)(3) status, EIN, mailing address, and contact information.
  • Dedicated transparency and privacy pages for visitor trust and policy compliance.
  • Program descriptions tied to education, social protection, poverty reduction, public policy, and cultural exchange.
  • Substack integration for fresh public-interest content and thought leadership.
